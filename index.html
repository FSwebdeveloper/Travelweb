TREKKING IN SIKKIM

Sikkim is one of best trekking destination in Northeast in India. The terrain offers a unique rugged experience for all adventure seekers. Sikkim’s mountain ranges offers delight to Trekkers and travel enthusiasts. The mountainous terrain of Sikkim provide space and time to experience rugged terrain of eastern Himalayas. Trekkers wander around lush green trails untouched natural splendor and Trekkers have the chance to sight many exotic animals like Deer and Red Panda. The state’s vast unspoiled mountain ranges are a trekker’s delight and its amazing range of flora and fauna is a dream come true for all nature lovers.
